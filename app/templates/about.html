ABOUT US

The Story Behind Looph

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Looph is about everyday pieces you actually reach for. Rooted in Manila and inspired by the Looph community, we design capsules that feel considered but never overdone. From fabric choice to fit, every drop is built around how you move through real life.

THE LOOPH VISION

Looph started as a small run of pieces made for friends and has grown with the people who wear them. We focus on breathable fabrics, thoughtful silhouettes, and details that photograph well but feel even better in person. Each collection is a snapshot of how our community styles the everyday.
